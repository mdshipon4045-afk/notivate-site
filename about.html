My Story

A few years ago, I was drowning. Between university assignments, side projects, part-time work, and trying to have a life — I couldn't keep track of anything. I tried every to-do app, every planner, every productivity system. None of them stuck.

Then I discovered Notion. And I broke it immediately — in the best possible way. I spent weeks building, breaking, and rebuilding systems inside Notion until I had something that actually reflected how my brain worked.

Friends started asking me to share my setups. Then strangers on Twitter. Then, after posting one template on Gumroad on a whim, I woke up to 30 sales overnight. That's when I realized: people don't just want to be organized — they want a system that's already built, that actually works, and that doesn't require a PhD in Notion to understand.

So I kept building. I refined every template obsessively — testing with real people, collecting feedback, tweaking layouts and databases until they felt effortless to use. Every template I release has been used by my own hands for weeks before it reaches you.

Notivate isn't just a template shop. It's a belief that good systems change lives — and you shouldn't need to be a Notion expert to have one.
